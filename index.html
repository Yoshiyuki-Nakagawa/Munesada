Alliance Guidelines

These rules are not strict laws. They are guidelines to keep the alliance friendly and safe.

General Rules
  1. Real life and work come first. Play Whiteout Survival only when you have free time.
  2. Do NOT attack non-enemy alliances. This includes NAP allies, friendly alliances, and alliances not in war with us. If an event allows attacks, follow the event rules. If you attack by mistake, talk to the Leader or R4 officers.
  3. World Chat: Jokes are OK, but no insults, no racism, no harassment. Your words represent our alliance.
  4. Respect everyone. All players are equal, no matter rank or alliance. If there is a conflict, talk first. Ask the Leader or officers if needed.
  5. Control anger. Some players act strange or rude. Do not act in anger. Talk to the Leader or officers if you feel upset.
  6. You can leave the alliance anytime. If possible, please talk to the Leader or officers first.
  7. Emergency: In urgent situations, act first. Please report to the Leader or officers later.
R2 Members (New Members)
  1. Try to grow your power to 1,000,000 (1M) if possible. When you reach 1M, you will be promoted to R3.
  2. Alliance tech donation: Up to 40 donations per day is enough for daily rewards. Extra donation is optional. Use resources to grow.
  3. Turn ON Auto Join in Alliance War menu.
R3 Members
  1. Help R2 members grow. For example, start monster rallies or small events.
  2. Donate more to alliance tech if you can.
  3. In war, send reinforcements to R2 members if you can.
R4 Members (Officers)
  1. Please also follow R3 rules.
  2. Do not overwork. Real life first. If you have time, please help with:
    1. Promote new members to R2.
    2. Promote R2 to R3 when power is over 1M.
    3. Start alliance tech research when resources are ready.
    4. Mediate conflicts when the Leader is absent.
    5. Build alliance flags for free resource land. If crossing another alliance land, talk to the Leader first.
    6. If Leader is away for over 3 days, discuss and appoint a Temporary Leader.
    7. If Leader disappears suddenly (hospital, accident, etc.):
      1. Hold officer meeting.
      2. Choose next Leader with member agreement. One officer pays 200 diamonds for leader transfer (discuss who pays)
R5 Member (Leader)
  1. Please also follow R3 and R4 rules.
  2. Negotiate with other alliances and apologize if needed. Stay calm in diplomacy.
  3. Contact other alliance Leaders by private chat first. If no response, ask in World Chat for a contact person.
  4. If Leader will be absent for over 3 days, explain reason and appoint Temporary Leader with officers. Avoid forcing people.
Penalties
  1. If a member is inactive for 21 days (3 weeks) without notice, officers may consider removing the member. This also applies to Leader and officers. If you notify us, this rule does not apply.
  2. If you attack non-enemy alliances on purpose or many times, and officers vote majority, you may be removed.
Additional Rules
  1. Share these rules by HTML or PDF. Post the URL in alliance notice. Use full-width characters for URLs or a short link (like x.gd).
  2. To change rules, use an alliance poll and get more than half approval.
Disclaimer
These rules are only for in-game community management. They do not create real-world legal obligations or responsibilities.
